Short version: Everything you enter stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

1. Overview

Task Tracker is a personal productivity app that helps you manage tasks, set reminders, and track priorities. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All task data (titles, priorities, reminders, completion status) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Permissions Used

Camera (android.permission.CAMERA)

  • Purpose: Allows you to take a photo to attach to a task.
  • What we do with it: The photo is saved to your device's temporary cache folder. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.
  • Optional: If denied, the app falls back to your gallery. All other features remain fully available.

Notifications (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

  • Purpose: Sends a local reminder notification at the time you set.
  • What we do with it: Notifications are generated entirely on-device. No content is transmitted externally.
  • Optional: In-app banners still appear when the app is open if denied.

Vibration (android.permission.VIBRATE)

  • Purpose: Vibrates the device when a reminder notification is delivered.
  • No data is collected.

Receive Boot Completed (android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED)

  • Purpose: Restores scheduled reminders after a device restart.
  • No data is read from or written to any external service.

5. Third-Party Services

Task Tracker does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data from this app is received by any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

Task Tracker does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no advertising, no account creation, and no data transmission of any kind.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual tasks using the Delete button inside the app, or
  • Going to Android Settings → Apps → Task Tracker → Storage → Clear Data, or
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all associated data from your device.

8. Security

All data remains on your device and is subject to Android's built-in application sandboxing. We do not transmit data over any network, so there is no risk of interception.
